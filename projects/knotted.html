This is one of my first sites after I graduated, the client really like the idea of a one page layout done diferent, so we agreed that a horizontal scroll would give her the uniqueness she wanted still keeping the site user friendly.

One of the most entertaining parts of this site was creating the artwork, Knotted Ribbon really wanted the site to be palpable, so I added element from the real world and used them to embelish the site as much as possible without compromising load time.

For this client I also created the logo and branding, the logo of course is composed of a ribbon with a small knot in it, and their branded color is royal purple to give her clients a first impression of quality.
